About The Bluebell

The Bluebell is an elegant three bedroom, two bathroom bungalow with a spacious open plan. It has an abundance of modern comforts all set on your own site with fantastic views that can be seen from the front and back of the house. The gracious layout caters to both distinct entertaining and private living with two bedrooms on one side, and the master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom & walk-in on the other side of the living areas.
